      Arise all Sons of the Mountaineer
      Sing praises to the Lord
      And shout for joy with one accord
      All Boys from far and near
      Remember now the glorious days
      At Oke-Are’s gates
      Whence we came and first sang our praise
      Deo et Patriae.
